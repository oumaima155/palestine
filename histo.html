Initially, Palestinians and Jews lived together in the same land. Over time, many Jews immigrated to other parts of the world, but some remained in Palestine. However, with the rise of Zionism, a political movement that sought to establish a Jewish homeland in Palestine, some Jews began to assert their claim to the land, arguing that it was their rightful territory based on religious beliefs. This ultimately led to the displacement of many Palestinian people from their homes and lands. Tragically, today Palestinians still face a constant threat of violence, including killings and torture
